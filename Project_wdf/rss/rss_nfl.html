New York Jets Acquire Tim Tebow

The New York Jets signed Quarterback Tim Tebow on March 21, 2012 from the Denver Broncos after a trade that sent him to New York when Denver acquired Peyton Manning from the Indianapolis Colts.

NFL punishes New Orleans Saints

The New Orleans Saints have been given penalties for the "bounty" program they instituted from 2009 to 2011. Many were shocked of the severity of this and the extent of this damage is unknown.

Quarterback Impresses

Quarterback Robert Griffin III, otherwise known as RG3, has impressed the Redskins and the Colts so far. He completed 78 of 84 throws at Baylor's Pro Day. The Colts look to pick Andrew Luck while the Washington Redskins may in fact, draft RG3 second in the upcoming NFL Draft.
