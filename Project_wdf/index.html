Tim Tebow

News

The New York Jets signed Quarterback Tim Tebow on March 21, 2012 from the Denver Broncos after a trade that sent him to New York when Denver acquired Peyton Manning from the Indianapolis Colts.

Biography

He is regarded as one of the greatest college players in the NCAA. Born in the Philippines, Tim Tebow is known for his option Quarterback play in the collegiate level and in the Pros. Known for his strong presence in his religious faith, he uses his passion to spread his Christianity. He was a home-schooled student and played high school football in Jacksonville, FL.

College Career

Accomplishing more than a College National Championship in college, Tim Tebow is regarded as a vocal and spirited individual player. His style of run and shoot offense has led his teams to recognized post season wins.

  1. Led the Florida Gators to 12-1 record in 2008 and the eventual National Championship.
  2. Won the Heisman Trophy*.
